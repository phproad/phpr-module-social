1. Go to https://www.facebook.com/developers/ and create a new application.
  2. Enter your application name (the name of your site will do). Leave App Namespace blank and Web Hosting unchecked. Click continue
  3. Enter the captcha and click Continue
  4. Enter in the App Domains field:
    get_hostname() ?>
  5. Click the section Website with Facebook Login and enter the following Site URL:
    get_callback_url() ?>
  6. Once you have Saved Changes, copy and paste the created App ID and App Secret into this setup page.
